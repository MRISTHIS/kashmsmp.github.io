These rules keep the Kashm SMP fun, fair, and safe for everyone.

1. General Rules

  • Do not beat the Ender Dragon
  • Only members of Konos High School or alumni allowed
  • No griefing or stealing.
  • No hacking.
  • No lag machines or intentional server disruption.
  • Respect all players.

2. Chat Rules

  • No harassment, hate speech, or slurs.
  • No spamming or flooding chat.
  • Keep chat friendly and appropriate.

3. Gameplay Rules

  • PvP is allowed only with consent or with valid reason.
  • No trapping players unfairly or in personal area.
  • No killing AFK players.
  • Respect claimed areas and builds.

4. Punishments

  • Warnings for minor offenses.
  • Temporary bans for repeated rule-breaking.
  • Permanent bans for hacking, griefing, or extreme behavior.
